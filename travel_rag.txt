Frequently Asked Questions
What is the best season for Himalayan treks?
Most Himalayan treks operate from May to October depending on altitude and snowfall conditions.
Is travel insurance mandatory?
Yes, travel insurance covering adventure activities is highly recommended.
Are permits included?
Protected area permits are included where specifically mentioned in package inclusions.
What happens during bad weather?
Alternative routes, buffer days, or activity rescheduling may be arranged based on local conditions.
